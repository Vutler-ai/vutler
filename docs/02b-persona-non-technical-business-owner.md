## Decision-Making

**Decision Criteria:**  
What factors influence tool/product choices?
1. **Ease of use:** "Can I set this up in 30 minutes?" - No coding, clear UI, guided onboarding
2. **Real-world utility:** "Will this actually DO things?" - Agent must email, chat, schedule (not just chat interface)
3. **Cost:** Flat fee <$100/month (Elena compares to Notion, Slack pricing)
4. **Testimonials:** "Do other business owners use this?" - Needs social proof (case studies, reviews)
5. **Support:** "Can I get help when stuck?" - Prefers chat support over "read the docs"

**Influence & Authority:**
- **Decision-maker:** Yes - Elena owns the business; full authority on software purchases <$500/month
- **Budget authority:** $50-500/month "software budget" (no board approval needed)
- **Who else influences:** Business coach, peer network (other SMB owners), YouTube influencers (business/AI channels)

**Adoption Barriers:**
- **Technical overwhelm:** If setup involves terminal, code, or complex config = abandoned
- **Trust:** Unknown startup = risky; prefers tools with testimonials, case studies, money-back guarantee
- **Time:** Elena is BUSY; if onboarding takes >1 hour = "I'll do this later" (never happens)
