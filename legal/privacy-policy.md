This Privacy Policy applies to the Vutler AI Agent Workspace platform ("Vutler", "Service") operated by Starbox Group GmbH ("we", "us", "our"). This policy governs the collection, processing, and protection of personal data in accordance with:

- Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (LPD)
- European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- Applicable international data protection laws

## 7. Data Retention Periods

### 7.1 Active Accounts
- Account data: Duration of service relationship
- Chat messages: 3 years from creation date
- Agent interactions: 1 year from interaction date
- Usage logs: 13 months from collection date
- Payment data: 10 years (legal requirement)

### 7.2 Deleted Accounts
- Account deletion: 30 days grace period, then permanent deletion
- Backup retention: Maximum 90 days post-deletion
- Legal hold data: Retained per legal requirements only

### 7.3 Anonymized Analytics
Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics may be retained indefinitely for product improvement.

### 8.3 Right to Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)

- Does not collect personal data
