## 2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They enable websites to remember information about your visit and preferences, improving functionality and user experience.

### 2.1 Types of Cookies
- **Session cookies:** Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies:** Stored on your device until expiration or manual deletion
- **First-party cookies:** Set by the website you're visiting (Vutler)
- **Third-party cookies:** Set by external services (not used by Vutler)

## 3. Cookies We Use

Vutler follows a privacy-by-design approach with minimal cookie usage. We only use cookies that are strictly necessary for platform functionality or have clear user consent.

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the platform to function properly and cannot be disabled:

**Authentication Cookies**
- **Name:** `vutler_session`
- **Purpose:** User authentication and session management
- **Duration:** Session (deleted when browser closes)
- **Data:** Encrypted session identifier

**Security Cookies**
- **Name:** `csrf_token`
- **Purpose:** Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) protection
- **Duration:** Session
- **Data:** Random security token

**Load Balancing Cookies**
- **Name:** `server_affinity`
- **Purpose:** Route requests to appropriate server
- **Duration:** Session
- **Data:** Server identifier (no personal data)

### 3.2 Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and settings:

**User Preferences**
- **Name:** `vutler_preferences`
- **Purpose:** Store UI preferences, language, theme settings
- **Duration:** 1 year
- **Data:** User interface preferences (anonymized)

**Accessibility Settings**
- **Name:** `accessibility_prefs`
- **Purpose:** Remember accessibility options
- **Duration:** 1 year
- **Data:** Accessibility settings (no personal identifiers)

### 3.3 Analytics Cookies

We use privacy-friendly analytics that do not track individuals:

**Umami Analytics**
- **Service:** Self-hosted Umami analytics
- **Purpose:** Understand usage patterns, improve service
- **Data Collected:** Page views, session duration, referrer information
- **Privacy Features:**
  - No personal data collection
  - No cross-site tracking
  - No user profiling
  - No data sharing with third parties
  - Respects Do Not Track headers
  - IP addresses are hashed and anonymized
- **Opt-out:** Automatically honors Do Not Track browser settings

## 5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### 5.1 Browser Settings
You can control cookies through your browser settings:

**Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data  
**Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data  
**Safari:** Settings > Privacy > Cookies and Website Data  
**Edge:** Settings > Cookies and site permissions

### 5.2 Cookie Preferences in Vutler
Access cookie preferences through:
- Account Settings > Privacy > Cookie Preferences
- Footer link: "Cookie Settings"

### 5.3 Impact of Disabling Cookies
- **Essential cookies:** Cannot be disabled; platform will not function
- **Preference cookies:** Settings will reset to defaults each session
- **Analytics cookies:** No impact on functionality

## 6. Do Not Track

- DNT enabled: Analytics cookies are automatically disabled
- No behavioral tracking regardless of DNT setting
- Essential cookies still function (required for security)
