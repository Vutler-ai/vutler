## FAQ

**Can I start with Community and upgrade later?**
Yes. Your data and configuration migrate seamlessly to any paid plan.

**Do I bring my own LLM API keys?**
Yes. Vutler connects to your existing LLM providers. You pay them directly. We don't mark up token costs.

**Is there a free trial of paid plans?**
14-day free trial on Pro and Team. No credit card required.

**What's the difference between Cloud and Dedicated hosting?**
Cloud runs on shared Swiss infrastructure. Dedicated gives you an isolated instance with your own Vaultbrix backend. Available on Enterprise.

**Can I self-host Enterprise features?**
Enterprise Edition (EE) is available for on-premise deployment under a commercial license. Contact sales.
